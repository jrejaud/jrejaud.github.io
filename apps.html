Charge Beam

Charge up and shoot your power beam! Compatible with Android Wear!

Hold the screen to charge up your shot! Release to shoot!

You can launch the app from your phone or use your Android Wear watch to charge shots (Android Wear is optional)

Pew pew! Scare your cat!

CleverObjects

Control SmartThings switches using voice commands and buttons on your watch.

CleverObjects lets you control your SmartThings switches and locks using voice command or buttons on your android wear watch.

Want to turn off the lights before you go to bed? Start CleverObjects and say "Turn off bedroom light and turn off bathroom light"

Munchkin Companion

Munchkin Companion is an unofficial companion app for the popular card game "Munchkin"(tm) by Steve Jackson

Keep track of each player's level and gear score.

Users can change max level from 10 to whatever they want (New feature)

Battle calculator keeps track of level, gear, and modifier score for you and the monster and determines the winner.

FalconPunch

As you pull back your fist to punch, unleash the FALCON PUNCH, or flick your wrist (to show them your moves), your phone will play very appropriate sounds.

Put on your Android Wear watch, load the app and SHOW THEM YOUR MOVES.
