Jordan Réjaud, EIT

I'm a Robotics Engineer living in San Antonio, Texas with a background in Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Computer Science. I'm also dabbling in website design, video editing, and android development. The learning page on my site is a catalogue of online resources I have used (and am currently using) to learn new things.

When I was younger, I knew I wanted to be an engineer, I wanted to build things and study the world around me. I didn't just want to know how to do things, I wanted to appreciate how things worked, the patterns and logic that an untrained eye can't put into focus. Robotnik from Sonic the Hedgehog is my idol.

I wanted (and still want) to learn everything of interest. In conjunction to my engineering studies, I also got minors in aerospace engineering, anthropology, and business.

I've designed and built an airplane, a prosthetic arm, a few robots, a hologram generator, and a bunch of other stuff!

You can send me an email, if you like.
