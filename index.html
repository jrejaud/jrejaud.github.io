Jordan Réjaud, EIT

I'm a Robotics Engineer living in San Antonio, Texas with a background in Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Computer Science. I'm also dabbling in website design, video editing, and Android development. The learning page on my site is a catalogue of online resources I have used (and am currently using) to learn new things.

When I was younger, I knew I wanted to be an engineer, I wanted to build things and study the world around me. Robotnik from Sonic the Hedgehog is my idol. I want to get little birds and use them to power big robots that go around bothering rapid Erinaceinae.

I'm an eclectic fellow, in conjunction to my Robotics Engineering studies, I also received minors in Aerospace Engineering, Anthropology, and Business.

I've designed and built an airplane, a prosthetic arm, a few robots, a hologram generator, and a bunch of other stuff!
